Secondary Emotions

  • Secondary emotions are learned from our environment and experiences, like guilt, embarrassment, pride, or shame.


    Psychology of Emotions

  • How to recognize emotions?

    • Happiness → Contentment, Excitement, Pride

    To foster happiness through contentment, excitement, and pride, practice gratitude, embrace new experiences, and celebrate personal achievements.

    Anger → Frustration, Annoyance, Rage

    To manage anger through frustration, annoyance, and rage, practice deep breathing, mindfulness, and constructive communication to express your feelings calmly.

    Fear → Anxiety, Nervousness, Worry

    To manage fear through anxiety, nervousness, and worry, use relaxation techniques, challenge negative thoughts, and focus on the present moment.

    Disgust → Contempt, Revulsion, Disapproval

    To manage disgust through contempt, revulsion, and disapproval, practice empathy, reframe the situation, and focus on understanding rather than judgment.
